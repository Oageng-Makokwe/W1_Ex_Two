Cockatiel

Cockatiels are one of the most popular bird breeds to own as a pet. They're friendly, playful, form strong bonds with their owners and are relatively easy to care for. Better yet, cockatiels can make long term companions. In fact, according to All Pet Bird Info, cockatiels can live for up to 20 years.

A cockatiel's Favorite Things in Order!

  1. Play with toys
  2. Chewing on wood
  3. Eating
